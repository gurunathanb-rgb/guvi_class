# Medicine Delivery App

This App is used to order the medicine online without going to the Medical store in person

## How to install the App

Download the Med App and install it in to your Android or Apple Phone

## How to Register
Open the App and register using your mobile or email ID with Address

## How to Order
- Login in to the App and search for medicine with the name
- Verify the name and the image shown before moving it to the Cart
- Enter the number of Qty Required
- Ensure the Address is your currently living address
## How to Pay
- After ordering click the check out button
- Select the payment method UPI or Net Banking
- Select the UPI and Bank Details

## How to track
- Once Payment completed sucessfully your order generated
- You can view and track the order
